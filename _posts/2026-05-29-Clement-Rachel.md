Clement Dunn and Rachel Bier have joined the group for Summer 2026 as part of the Physics Department's REU site program at Lehigh University.  Clement joins us from Northwestern University and will be working on designing custom direct digital synthesizers radio-frequency generation. Rachel joins us from Wellesley College and wll be working on designing bitter-style electromagnets for generation of tunable magnetic fields for addressing Feshbach resonances. Welcome Rachel and Clement! We are happy to have you here!
