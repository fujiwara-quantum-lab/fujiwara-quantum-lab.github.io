# Research
The lab is currently building our first experiment as we await completeion of lab renovations. Our experimental platform will consist of ultracold Bose and Fermi gases inside 2D and 3D optical lattices.  Future experiments will focus on quantum simulation of multi-band Hubbard models, Feshbach interactions, disordered-mediated phenomena, and non-equilbrium dyanmics. Stay tuned as this page develops as we develop our research program!
